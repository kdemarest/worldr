**Military Engineer**

**Cartographer**  
Aaron Arrowsmith [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipOhaxlszFgIzU7zFXfe1Gvh8uuKFLoyh4Td3qoY)@ \- Renowned British cartographer famous for his detailed maps of the world, with a focus on North America and the Pacific.

**Astronomer**  
John Herschel \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipPVLduQ31BY5lhbGfat8GTHlB68U9PQIEtxBXgJ)@ A leading British astronomer who made extensive studies of the southern hemisphere's stars and contributed to the development of photography.

**Archaeologist \- Lost left Arm**  
Jean-François Champollion (French) \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipPbSchKxBAiBmAPeGkp56H__QprirsUONvh17R8)@ Deciphered the Rosetta Stone, paving the way for modern Egyptology.

**5 Artist and Illustrator**  
J. M. W. Turner \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipMmiFDawHRWrQADlLmu-cjmCC0tmL972Xw4nOZI)@ Celebrated British painter known for his expressive landscapes and seascapes, pushing the boundaries of 19th-century art.

**Botanist**  
William Jackson Hooker \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipPC0j79CUiPP47fCuU4ait7ovB4mQNeM5n-3Ey1)@ Eminent British botanist who became the first Director of the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, and significantly expanded its collection.

**Zoologist**  
Georges Cuvier (French) \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipPhCHTuMfbTJrnLzykJQeaKLjLAmxOytf2qDpTV)@ A founder of paleontology, known for establishing extinction as a fact and for his work in comparative anatomy.

**Geologist**  
William Smith \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipP-DA_KY6okhcwktMd1IYl1Q6NXdgDkEwiFB0EG)@ Known as the "Father of English Geology," he created the first nationwide geological map.

**Physician**  
Astley Cooper \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipONsW6RxF7Gg2WGkp-fguA9s9VGgtiie_VHC10Q) An influential British surgeon and anatomist, known for his work on the anatomy of the breast and thymus gland.

**10 Diagnostician \- DEAD**  
**Jean-Nicolas Corvisart (French)** \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipMCQQAZ81Wm3JhaRPk5bOA1J6wI8mSuhQdlDRCA) Lung and heart specialist best known as Napoleon Bonaparte's personal doctor and valued advisor. Pioneer in the use of chest percussion as a diagnostic tool.

**Surgeon \- left leg lost up to the body**  
John Hunter \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipME_dTZLLQv-4WwQzRuqhvtgsFSMHEHs5RwSJOX)@ Scottish. The father of modern surgery and a strict empiricist,, Hunter made substantial contributions to anatomy, pathology, and surgical techniques. 

**Pharmacologist**  
Pierre-Joseph Pelletier (French) \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipMdEOMoFE8r68jEs1NorXo8aNY2N2Mr74GVbFl6)@ Co-discovered chlorophyll and was a pioneer in isolating active ingredients from plants.

**Chemist**  
Humphry Davy \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipNJtsIefAvYJ1oqW8Cwdu8oIHkemzd3_U4tWaCD)@ Famous for discovering several chemical elements and the invention of the Davy lamp.

**Historian**  
Thomas Carlyle \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipM_sMRDXMkSHoUyHfsdCPms7QUcGqVpP1thxDJX)@ A Scottish philosopher and historian, known for his works on the French Revolution.

**15 Linguist \- Dead \- Back as a Spirit**  
Jacob Grimm (German) \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipOejVFXBgmwhkCD9qh3TGqYZ-1yXFoA2X9tz57c)@ One of the Grimm brothers, known for their fairy tales but also a key figure in the development of linguistic studies.

**Translator and Interpreter**  
Sarah Austin \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipPW8lodndPLbWQaQvVfOszLpIfObR0WqIi-JjeX) A renowned British translator of German texts, contributing significantly to the transmission of German culture to England.

**Phrenologist \- right arm up to the elbow**  
Franz Joseph Gall (German) \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipOkzFFbUBoPCblIqDTq0zMn6QlQr0dr-NIhAZBn) A neuroanatomist and physiologist who founded phrenology, claiming to determine character, personality traits, and criminality based on skull shape.

**Alchemist \- lost left hand**  
Johann Wolfgang Döbereiner \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipM7XSl0OuDLnch3FQkmn48pSbsnhYLL-giTeov1)@ Known for his formulation of the Law of Triads, classifying elements into groups based on their chemical properties, a foundation for the periodic table; platinum as a catalyst; acetone; hydrogen gas lamp

Antoine Levoisier \- [PIC](https://photos.google.com/album/AF1QipOTCRxzAnMfLbdoomC6wG7EACkwMejJcUYW5-QD/photo/AF1QipOTMZgwWDebzcow-0m46TYiqvPaHUbIX-KJxZKY) Thought executed during the French Revolution, in fact, Talleyrand had him smuggled from Paris to London, where he has worked for the Foreign Office ever since.
