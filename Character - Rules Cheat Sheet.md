**Statistics (pg 207\)**

* Stat		\= Might, Speed, Intellect  
* Pool		\= Spendable stat points, always reduced by Edge  
* Edge		\= Reduces cost of Effort, per stat pool  
* Trades		\= At creation may adjust edge, pool, cypher for skill, weapon training for skill  
  		one \-1 edge for \+1 edge; one inability for skill; 1 std ability for 1 flavor ability

**Character**

* Descriptor	\= I am \<flavor\> \<type\> who \<focus\>  
* Abilities		\= In Tiers T1-T6; some cost pool; may be actions or enablers  
* Skills		\= Practiced \+0, Trained \+1, Specialized \+2; may be an ability

**Progression**

* Tiers		\= You grow 1 tier per 16 xp spent (eg 4 Benefits gained)  
* Advancement	\= 4xp buys (+4 pool pts / \+1 edge / \+1 effort / 1 skill) or an Option; pg240  
* Options		\= gain ability or focus, \+1 armor speed, \+2 to recovery rolls  
* LT Benefits	\= a contact, home, title, job, wealth, pg239  
* Experience	\= 1+1 at GM Intrusion, 2 arc progress, discovery, artifacts; pg238  
* Arc		\= character growth steps; first is free, then \-1xp for each additional

**Actions**

* Difficulty	\= How hard the Action to complete a Task is.

			Retry is OK, but *must* use at least 1 level of Effort.

* Inability		\= 1 Hinder  
* Asset		\= A circumstance that gives you 1 to 2 Ease  
* Effort Stat	\= The max levels of effort one can apply to a single action

  *\*\* must declare effort spend prior to the roll*

* Effort (diff)	\= Uses edge+pool pts to reduce difficulty L1=3, L2=5, L3=7  
* Effort (dmg)	\= \+3 damage per level of effort (or \+2 for AoE)  
* Armor		\= decrease damage received by armor  
* Spend Limit	\= Edge may be spent on ONE effort only (to hit or damage)

**Rolling**  
**1d20 vs 3x ( Difficulty \+ 0-1Hinder \- 0-2Skill \- 0-2Assets \- Effort (Pool-Edge3:5:7) )**

* Roll1		\= GM intrusion (mishap) typically personal to the roller  
* Roll17		\= \+1 damage  
* Roll18		\= \+2 damage  
* Roll19		\= \+3 damage or a status effect  
* Roll20		\= \+4 damage or dramatic effect  
* Combat Effect	\= Attempt a Minor effect at \-1, and \-4 dmg; Major effect at \-2, \-8 dmg

**Damage**

* Applies to Might \-\> Speed \-\> Intelligence  
  State		Pools \<= 0	Effort Penalty  
  Hale		0		0  
  Impaired	1		1/LoE; No minor/major effects; extra dmg=1  
  Debilitated	2		No actions; May only crawl

**Recovery**

* Recover	\= Each rest add 1d6+tier to your pools, however you like

**General**

* Cypher		\= a one-time use ability, either innate or from an item  
* Intrusion	\= GM introduces complication; player accept \+1+1xp, reject \-1xp

			eg a complication, fumble, bad coincidence, twists of fate, mixed success

* Turn		\= the Action unit, equal to 1/6th of a minute  
* Distance	\= immediate 10, short 50, long 100, very long 1000
