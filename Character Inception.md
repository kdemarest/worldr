# Character Inception

To generate your character fill in the following elements:

Name:

Role:

Background:

Where did they grow up?

What were their parents like?

Who (or what) did they lose, that mattered to them?  

What are they proud of?

What defines their identity?

What do they fear deeply?

What is their addiction/obsession/compulsion?

What social class? What magnitude of wealth?

What are some capabilities got from your upbringing?

What "delicate arts" do you have, for example were you trained in painting, an instrument, dance, cooking, witty repartee, etc?

What is their role-playable character flaw?

What are you running from or in what way unsuited to British life?

What practical expeditionary skill are you cross-trained in? This training can be before the voyage, or during a short period just before and during the voyage.
