Navigator John "Beard" Twomey \- [PIC](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/41/Joule_James_Jeens_engraving.jpg) Known for his waist-length bushy beard, people just call him beard. Grew up on the sea side with a father who was former Navy. He is a harsh critic of anyone not acting "proper" on the ship, despite his non-regulation beard. A skilled gambler.

Weatherman Zadkiel Tao-Sze aka Richard Morrison \- [PIC](http://PIC) An inveterate astrologer and the official predictor of the weather, he studies astro-meteorology and writes in his Zadkiel's Almanac, which he future-dates as to when the events will happen.

Cook Francesco "Greasy" del Mondo \- [PIC](https://www.berfrois.com/uploads/2011/12/treasure-island.jpg) A demanding and precise Italian who makes excellent meals, is handy with a knife, and always dragooning somebody to help him prepare the meals.

Boatswains x2 \- Davey Chilton [PIC](https://www.warhistoryonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/thomas_rowlandson-seaman-1-413x640.jpg) and Davey Coxshammer [PIC](https://i.pinimg.com/474x/78/4c/f2/784cf215d3c479c44721ef20e4e2093b--maritime-museum-the-sailor.jpg), junior officers who maintain the ropes, sails, longboats, and both operating and giving orders to operate the fore and aft sails, and launch and manage the longboats. Known as the Daveys, and very very close friends, one is from Norwich and the other from Penzance. "About as far apart as can be\!" they argue, trying to stop being lumped together all the time. But nobody listens. They are the Daveys. 

Cooper Fredrick "Coops" Figgleston \- [PIC](https://i.imgur.com/sk1xnAP.jpg) Maintains the barrels containing all food. He is large, and strong, and extremely attractive. When he is working he does so without a shirt, and everyone is always impressed.

Quartermaster Terrence Merryweather \- [PIC](https://i.imgur.com/YMOjzNO.jpeg) A rather dry but very efficient young man. He has dreams of adventure, but it more temperamentally suited to adding numbers.

Quartermasters Quentin Hampling and Charlie Smith \- Maintain the cargo hold, loading and unloading, the winches, fishing supplies, the harpoons, and the hours of standing watch.

Carpenter Jules "Ruby" du Champs \- [PIC](https://i.pinimg.com/originals/e7/75/89/e77589e307abe52b7e5372fdd3007bbc.jpg) A French expatriate and royalist who maintains the decks, masts, hull, and produces or repairs all the wooden objects on the boat. The nickname is because a Ruby is a Jewel, and his name is Jules.
