## **Jungle Flora**

1\. [Siamese Cassia](https://ideasforus.org/traditional-medicinal-edible-plants-in-ghana/)	Leaves remove intestinal **worms**, prevent **seizures**  
		Bark salve cures **scabies**

2\. [Guajava](https://ideasforus.org/traditional-medicinal-edible-plants-in-ghana/)		Leaves reduce **asthma** and breathing issues, and **diarrhea**

3\. [Moringa](https://ideasforus.org/traditional-medicinal-edible-plants-in-ghana/)		Beans are extra-healthful, decreasing by 1 any harm to pool or edge

4\. [Mondia](https://ideasforus.org/traditional-medicinal-edible-plants-in-ghana/)		Stems make excellent rope  
			Root paste causes sleep

5\. Cinchona		Anti-malarial
