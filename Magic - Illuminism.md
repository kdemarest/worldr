# Illuminism

Spirits are "made of" light. This magic has to do with enabling tethered spirits to manifest as pure light (and sometimes sound), to make images, illusions or focused beams as you command.

* T1 Eyes Adjusted (e)  
* T1 Minor Illusion (i1)  
* T2 Cast Illusion (e)  
* T3 Burning Light (i3) \*5 dmg\*  
* T3 Center of Attention  
* T4 Sunlight \- per CSR  
* T6 Grandiose Illusion (i8)
