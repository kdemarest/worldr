# Mentalism

A mage can use a tethered spirit to speak directly to a living spirit in order to influence it, bypassing the protections of the sentient mind.

Abilities:

* Find an Opening  
* **T1 Telepathic** (i1+) - per CSR  
* **T1 Mental Link** (i1) - per CSR  
* **T1 Calm Stranger** (i2+) - per CSR  
* **T2 Silent Goad** (i1) - per Goad except they don't know why they thought of it.  
* **T2 Link Senses** (i2) - per CSR  
* **T2 See the Unseen** (i2) - per CSR but on other people  
* **T2 Cloud Personal Memories** (i3) - per CSR  
* **T3 Infer Thoughts** (i4) - per CSR  
* **T4 Use Senses of Others** (i4) - per CSR  
* **T4 Mind Control** (i6+) - per CSR. This extremely prone to Black Marks.
* **T6 Change the Paradigm** (i6+)  
* **T6 Word of Command** (i6+cypher6) - per CSR
