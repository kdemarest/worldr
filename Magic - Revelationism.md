# Revelationism

Spirits: Light or Dark

Calling upon spirits to **sense** something or **reveal** information When a spirit is required to travel as part of such magic, the GM will determine the time necessary.

Abilities:

* **T1 Read Aura** (i1) - you read the spirit aura of an item, place, or person. Big events and traumas are easier to read. The result might yield insight and be a feeling, vision, whisper, smell or taste. This might create an asset for another task. Action.  
* **T1 Sense Ambush** (e) - per CSR  
* **T1 Spirit Sensor** (i1) - You send a spirit to one place within short range, where it will stay for one minute. You may look from its eyes for one minute or end at will.  
* **T1 See the Unseen** (i2) - per CSR but on other people  
* **T1 Speaker for the Dead (i2+)** - per CSR, except with a spirit. If the corpse is not present the cost is i4+.  
* **T2 See History (i4)** - per CSR  
* **T2 Spirit Vision** (i1) - You may see what one tethered spirit sees for one minute, within short range  
* **T3 Spirit Eyes** (i3)  - You may see what one tethered spirit sees for ten minutes, within one mile  
* **T3 Awareness** (i3) - per CSR; For ten minutes you are aware of all living and dead spirits within long range, including general position. By taking another action you can attempt to learn basic stats about a chosen spirit.  
* **T3 Spirit Location** (e) - per CSR Echolocation; You sense the latent spirits in nearby objects.  
* **T3 Trapfinder** (i3+) - per the CSR; A spirit does the finding.  
* **T3 Spirit Chat** (i3) - chat for one minute with any spirit you can perceive and communicate with.  
* **T3 Infer Thoughts** (i4) - per CSR  
* **T4 Distance Viewing** (i5) - per CSR; A tethered spirit is an immobile sensor at any distance it can travel where you have already been, for one hour.  
* **T4 Spirit Search** (i6+) - per CSR Experienced Finder; Your tethered spirits search for things on your behalf. They never feel bored or overworked by this activity.  
* **T4 Inhabit Crystal** - per CSR  
* **T4 Remote Viewing** - per CSR; A spirit travels to the place.  
* **T4 Spirit Sensing** - see a short distance through any obscuring phenomenon like fog, blindness. This is more real time and all purpose than just 'looking through walls'.  
* **T5 Spirit Companion** (e) - Choose one spirit as your companion. You can speak with it as if with a living, sentient person.  
* **T5 Amplify Sounds** (i2) - per CSR  
* **T5 True Senses**  
* **T5 Spirit Discourse (i4)** - Hold a full conversation with any spirit you can communicate with, up to ten minutes long.  
* **T6 Information Gathering (i5)** - per CSR; Except it is spirits  
* **T6 Spirit Companion (e)** - Choose one tethered spirit. It becomes untethered and regains free will. You may inhabit this spirit as possession any time. Your body will collapse, and still requires food etc.
