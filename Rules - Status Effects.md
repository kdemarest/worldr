# Status Effects

## Major Dark Effects
* Pariah (spoke or heard corrupt secrets)
* Morphed - melded with a beast
* Devolved - turned into an earlier hominid
* Invaded - controlled briefly by another; quicker and more resistable than possession
* Grumbled - distracted by an intrusive voice, leading to hindrance
* Possessed - controlled by an inhabiting spirit
* Inhabited - a dark magic user has taken up residence in one's mind solely to watch and listen
* Cursed - a shackled spirit interferes with one's body (spectrism) or mind (mentalism)

# General Status Effects
* Whispered - hearing things that aren't there
* Distracted - likely to miss the more important thing
* Shrouded - you do not see a thing that is present
* Enshrouded - you are hidden from another's sight
* Obstructed - hindered by involuntary clumsiness
* Frightened - causes one to stop, potentially shaking or dropping things; or to back away, possibly ignoring danger behind
* Queasy - like being kicked in the privates or punched in the gut, like an illness; requires 1 Effort to overcome
* Deafen - somewhat, like a person putting a finger in your ear  
* Chilled - causes shivering, sometimes uncontrollably, leading to dropping things or stuttering movement

# Game Effects

* Mishap - a manifestation of the malefaction accrued by a Pariah
