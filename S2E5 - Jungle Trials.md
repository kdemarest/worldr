## **To the River** 

[River Map](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gyimi_River#/media/File:Pra_OSM.jpg)

*Arriving at the Pra river delta, the sea is rough and the wind strong. The ship maneuvers carefully to stay in deep water, then weighs anchor. Your team gathers, with Bean, to enter the longboats. Avoiding the rocky shore, you must travel two miles up the river to a Ga village where you may commence the next phase of your journey.*

GM: Roll 4 sneaky/perception: *Bean's silent dwarfish companion is speaking with the quartermasters, arranging to lift something from the hold.* 

*The sea is high. Surging swells top the height of a grown man. The strong wind whips the wave crests into a blowing, eye-stinging spray. Bright sun illuminates the rocking ship, but fog obscures the shore in a dim haze of gray shades. The team steps into the longboat. The order is given by boatswain Chilton to lower away.*

GM: **Roll 3** to avoid throwing up in the swells. Assign who is rowing, and who looks ahead. The boatswain commands.

*Your longboat is deep within the trough between the man-high waves, surrounded by black sparkling water. For just a moment, it tops the swell.* 

GM: Those looking behind **Roll 3** to see:  *At the peaks of the wave you can see the ship you've left behind. Sailors scurry along the rigging, hauling something wide and round above the tossing deck. It glitters in the sun. The figure of Mr. Mump stands on the aftcastle.*

GM: **Roll 3** when a sudden wave strikes the boat to obey Chilton's orders and avoid swamping the boat. If swamped, all must bail and gear will be soggy.

*Your craft enters the shroud of fog. Behind, the ship is gone. Ahead, for the brief moment that the longboat tops the swell, the shore line is barely visible. Fiercely blowing salt spray mixes with tears in the eyes of all who look forward, blurring the scene.*

GM: **Roll 3** to spot: *Your stinging eyes spot a dark shape in the water, perhaps 100 yards away. Like a huge rock that dashes an errant ship, it rises from the water, then rises some more. This is no rock. The hulking shape moves towards your struggling craft.*

ALL: *A bright light pierces the fog above, blinding everyone in a sudden flash of illuminated fog. Squinting your eyes shut, the relentless brightness persists as the longboat dips into a trough, then rises again. A hissing noise erupts nearby, a large splash, and the blinding glare relents. The whipping wind brings a scent of scorched seaweed and flesh to your nostrils.*

GM: **Roll 3** again for another sudden wave. If already swamped, the boat will be overwhelmed and the PCs must make for shore however possible. Equipment will be lost.

*When you reach the mouth of the river the swells grow longer and calmer. Loud jungle noises compete with the calming sea. In a short while, you arrive at the Ga village, a cluster of twelve huts circling a central dirt plaza. A triplet of large, wood-carved statues* [PIC](https://p1.liveauctioneers.com/6938/180845/91007052_1_x.jpg?auto=webp&format=pjpg&quality=80&version=1601918189) *stand guard at the water's edge, where a number of fishing canoes rest.*
