# Jupiter {#jupiter}

Remember that Jupiter contains numerous swallowed planetoids and asteroids, former homes of the two "unknown" sentient species in the list below.

Important: The Corruptor **does have limits** to its power. It shackled all the spirits in each storm, but since it can't directly control so many it convinced them all, with threats and consequences when they were first shackled, to obey and relies on their beliefs and despair to keep them in check.[^1]

The changes in Victoria are due to the activities of the Mariners.

* The Mariners are not the specific organic creatures that everyone sees.  
* They are instead the cybernetics themselves, composed of both meccano electrical and biological aspects.  
* They have reproduced one of themselves into Victoria, a symbiotic sophont interweaving. That has heightened her intelligence, and her ability to see an objective reality. They chose her because she is both Obscura and has very strong intrinsic Spirit Mastery potential.  
* When all is said and done she will be given a choice about whether to retain this symbiotic relationship.  
* She is likely to choose to give her mother the experience of raising a normal child, and to take her place at the head of the British Empire.
